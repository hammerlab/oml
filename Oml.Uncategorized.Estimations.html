Module Oml.Uncategorized.Estimations

module Estimations: sig .. end

Methods to estimate derivatives of functions.
val secant : (float -> float) -> float -> float
secant f estimates the derivative of f by evaluating a the slope of a secant between the desired point and one close by (ie. (x,f(x)) and (x+h,f(x+h))). Errors are O(h).
val second_order : (float -> float) -> float -> float
second_order f estimates the derivatrive of f by evaluating the line connecting two points around x, specifically (x-h,f(x-h)) and (x+h,f(x+h)). Errors are O(h^2).
